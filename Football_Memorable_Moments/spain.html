Spain - 2010 World Cup


The 2010 World Cup was a special occassion. It marked the first time that Spain had won a World Cup. It also lamented a period of Spanish National team dominance in which Spain won the World Cup and European Championships on both sides of the world cup. By accomplishing this Spain became the first and only nation to win European National Championships on either sides of a World Cup.

Spanish squad celebrating World Cup triumph

This catapulted this generation of Spanish talents into the realm of the other great national teams such as the Zidane-led French team, the Pele-led Brazillian side as well as the Brazil squad consisting of Ronaldo, Ronaldinho and Rivaldo.
